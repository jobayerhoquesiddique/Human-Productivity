## Purpose
This directory is standardized to support efficient research-paper writing. All assets are grouped by academic function and named with a consistent convention.

## Folder Structure (Google Drive Standard)
- 00 Admin: administrative notes, structure guide
- 01 Project Overview/Proposal: proposals and hypotheses
- 01 Project Overview/Background: theoretical foundations and background
- 02 Literature Review: literature reviews and paper registries
- 03 Methodology: research methodology and methods
- 04 Data: datasets, registries, survey responses
- 05 Models: framework and model specifications
- 06 Results: outcomes, evaluation results
- 07 Figures: labeled figures and screenshots
- 08 Notebooks: Jupyter notebooks
- 09 Writing: paper outline and drafts
- 99 Archive: duplicates or deprecated items
- 10 Index & Catalogs: mapping and catalog files

## Naming Rules
- Documents and data: `slug_type_vNN.ext`
  - Example: `affective-computing_methodology_v01.docx`
- Figures: `fig-XX_caption_DATEorVNN.ext`
  - Example screenshot: `fig-03_data-columns-heuristic-matching_2026-02-03.png`
  - Example diagram: `fig-20_distributed-multimodal-framework_diagram_v01.png`

## Slug Rules
- Lowercase, hyphen-separated
- Remove extra punctuation
- Standardize spelling: `multimodal`
- Fix typos like `aProductivity` -> `productivity`

## Index Files
- 10 Index & Catalogs/filename-map.csv: old path to new path mapping
- 10 Index & Catalogs/figure-10 Index & Catalogs.csv: figure id, caption, and metadata
- 10 Index & Catalogs/asset-catalog.csv: master list of assets
